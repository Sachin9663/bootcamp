TO THE NEW organizes GeekCombat, a 2-day hackathon

The first edition of GeekCombat was co-sponsored by CarDekho, Daffodil Software and #fame. GeekCombat focused on Smarter India theme also served as a collaborative platform to learn and network.

TO THE NEW launches Wear Camcorder, a smart watch enabled camera app

The unique app developed by TO THE NEW works seamlessly on systems with Android 4.1 and above and is integrated with an advanced function that syncs with any smartphone of the specified configuration and can be easily installed on the Android smartwatch.
